Goals and Passions

Living life intentionally

I began mountain biking as a freshman in highschool. It changed my life in the sense that it drove me to become better. I learned that we each need something in life that causes us to grow. We each need to set goals in order to reach our full potential. Often, we don't truly understand our personal limits until we try to break them. I also learned that reaching our potential is one of the most fulfilling feelings in this world.

I strongly feel like life can easily pass us by. It is easy to get caught up in the day to day tasks that we forget to make time to truly live. It is so easy to live without being intentional. I think that is the reason why hobbies and pass times exist. It forces us to intentionally set time aside to do what we love.

Take some time today to set a BIG goal! Think of something that will encourage you to push your limits and become the best version of yourself!
